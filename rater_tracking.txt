========================================================
         FOLLOW-UP SCHEDULE REMINDERS
========================================================

Day 0    Send invitation email with materials and deadline.

Day 2    Send a friendly check-in if no acknowledgment received.
         ("Just checking you received the materials - let me know
          if you have any questions!")

Day 4-5  Send a mid-point reminder.
         ("Friendly reminder - the deadline is in [X] days.
          Let me know if you need anything!")

Day 6    Send a final reminder (1 day before deadline).
         ("The deadline is tomorrow. Please submit your completed
          spreadsheet whenever you are ready!")

Day 7    Deadline. Follow up with any outstanding raters individually.

Day 8+   If no response, decide whether to extend deadline or find
         a replacement rater.
